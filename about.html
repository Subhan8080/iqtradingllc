About IQ General Trading L.L.C.

Learn more about our company, mission, and vision

Who We Are

IQ General Trading L.L.C. is a Dubai-based trading company with a strong presence in the import/export and general trading sector. Founded with a vision to connect international manufacturers with local and regional markets, we have established ourselves as a reliable and professional trading partner.

Our company is registered and operates from Office No. 102, Redha Hassan Mararr Building, Al Murarr, Dubai, UAE. We serve businesses across the UAE and international markets by providing dependable sourcing, supply, and trading solutions.

Our Expertise

With years of professional trading experience, we specialize in:

  • Import Services: Sourcing quality products from international manufacturers
  • Export Services: Facilitating exports to regional and international markets
  • Local Trading: Supplying products to businesses within the UAE
  • General Order Supply: Meeting custom procurement requirements
  • Product Sourcing: Connecting businesses with the right suppliers
  • Logistics Support: Ensuring efficient delivery and supply chain management
