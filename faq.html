Common Questions

General Questions

What is IQ General Trading L.L.C.?

IQ General Trading L.L.C. is a Dubai-based trading company specializing in import, export, local trading, and general order supply. We connect international manufacturers with local and regional markets, providing reliable sourcing and supply solutions to businesses across the UAE and beyond.

Where is your office located?

Our office is located at Office No. 102, Redha Hassan Mararr Building, Al Murarr, Dubai, UAE. We are conveniently situated in Dubai, making it easy for local clients to visit us.

How long have you been in business?

We have years of professional trading experience and established relationships with suppliers and customers across the UAE and international markets. Our experience ensures reliable service and quality products.

What markets do you serve?

We serve businesses across the United Arab Emirates, the Middle East region, and international markets including Asia-Pacific and Europe. Our network allows us to facilitate trade across multiple regions.

About Our Services

What services do you offer?

We offer six main services:

  • Import Services
  • Export Services
  • Local Sales & Distribution
  • General Order Supply
  • Product Sourcing
  • Logistics & Supply Chain Management

Each service can be customized to meet your specific business needs.

Can you handle custom procurement requests?

Yes, absolutely! We specialize in custom procurement solutions. Whether you need products with specific specifications, custom quantities, or hard-to-find items, our team will work with you to source exactly what you need.

How quickly can you respond to inquiries?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. For urgent matters, please mention this in your message, and we will prioritize your request accordingly.

Do you provide logistics services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive logistics and supply chain management services including warehouse management, freight forwarding, customs clearance, tracking, and timely delivery to ensure smooth operations.

About Our Products

What types of products do you supply?

We supply products across multiple categories including:

  • Industrial Tools
  • Electrical Equipment
  • Construction Materials
  • Chemical Products
  • Machinery Parts & Accessories
  • Packaging Materials

We can also source products beyond these categories based on client requirements.

How do you ensure product quality?

We conduct rigorous quality inspections at the source and upon receipt of products. All products are sourced from certified manufacturers and meet international standards. We provide product warranties and customer protection guarantees.

Can you source products that are not listed?

Yes, we have an extensive international supplier network and can source virtually any product based on your specifications. Contact us with your requirements, and we'll identify the best suppliers and pricing for you.

Do you offer product certifications?

Yes, all our products come with necessary certifications and comply with international standards. We can provide documentation for product certifications upon request.

Pricing & Payment

How are your prices determined?

Our pricing is competitive and based on current market rates, product type, quantity, and supplier costs. We provide transparent pricing and are happy to negotiate bulk discounts for large orders. Request a quote from our team for specific pricing.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes, we offer significant discounts for bulk orders. The exact discount depends on product type, quantity, and specifications. Contact us with your bulk order requirements for a customized quote.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, letters of credit (L/C), and other secure payment methods. Payment terms can be negotiated based on your requirements and order size. Contact us to discuss payment options.

What is your minimum order quantity?

Minimum order quantities vary depending on the product. For some products, we accept small orders, while others have higher minimums. Contact us with your product requirements to confirm MOQ and pricing.

Do you offer flexible payment terms?

Yes, we can negotiate flexible payment terms for established business relationships and large orders. Payment options can include upfront payment, 50/50 split, or credit terms. Discuss your specific needs with our team.

Delivery & Logistics

What are your delivery timeframes?

Delivery timeframes depend on the product type, location, and whether it's imported or local supply. For local supplies in UAE, we typically deliver within 2-5 business days. International shipments may take 2-6 weeks depending on destination.

Do you provide tracking information?

Yes, we provide complete tracking information for all shipments. You'll receive tracking numbers and can monitor your order status throughout the delivery process.

Do you handle customs clearance?

Yes, we handle all customs clearance procedures and documentation for imports and exports. Our experience ensures smooth clearance and compliance with all regulations.

What are your shipping options?

We offer multiple shipping options including air freight, sea freight, and ground transportation depending on your urgency and budget. Contact us to discuss the best option for your shipment.

Business Partnerships

Can we establish a long-term partnership?

Absolutely! We are interested in building long-term partnerships with reliable clients. Long-term relationships often come with better pricing, priority service, and customized solutions. Contact us to discuss partnership opportunities.

Do you provide references from past clients?

Yes, we can provide references and testimonials from satisfied clients upon request. These references can help you understand the quality of our service and reliability.

Can you work on exclusive agreements?

We are open to discussing exclusive distribution or supply agreements with qualifying partners. Contact our team to discuss the details and benefits of an exclusive partnership.

How do I become a supplier or partner?

If you're a manufacturer or supplier interested in partnering with us, please contact us directly with your company information and product details. We'll discuss potential collaboration opportunities.
